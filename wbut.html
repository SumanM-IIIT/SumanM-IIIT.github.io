Life @WBUT:

 I started off my engineering life with this college. I got a good rank in the WBJEE-2012 entrance exam and chose WBUT to study B.Tech in Information Technology. Those days were undoubtedly the best days of my life. From academics to all other extra-curricular activities, it made me what I am today. I have got life-long friends there. There was pressure in academics but it all got balanced out with the fun and enjoyment we did. I also got a job on-campus in one of the largest MNCs of our country.

 The professors were really good, knowledgable and helpful. They actually pulled my attention towards Computer Science and engineering in general. The college campus was comparatively small but well maintained. The playgrounds, cafeteria, gymnasium, swimming pool etc everything was top notch which is rare for a government college.

 All-in-all it was a great experience. I have got so many memories there which I still cherish. There are a lot of memories related to my college life which I will share in this page gradually.
